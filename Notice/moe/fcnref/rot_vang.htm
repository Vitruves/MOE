Rotation Functions: rot3d_vAngle, rot3d_vBisector

Syntax

angle = rot3d_vAngle [u,v]
w = rot3d_vBisector [u,v]

Description

angle = rot3d_vAngle [u,v]

Returns the angle, in radians, between vectors u and v. angle describes the amount of rotation counterclockwise about the axis uxv (cross product) needed to make u coincident with v. u and v cannot be lists of vectors.

svl> rot3d_vAngle [ [1,0,0], [1,1,0] ]
0.785398

w = rot3d_vBisector [u,v]

Returns the bisector of vectors u and v. u and v cannot be lists of vectors.

svl> rot3d_vBisector [ [1,0,0], [0,1,0] ]
[0.707107,0.707107,0]
