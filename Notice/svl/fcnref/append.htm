Reshaping Functions: append, prepend

Syntax

result = append [v, w]
result = prepend [v, w]

Description

append inserts w at the end of v, and prepend inserts it at the front of v. The length of v and the level of nesting of w are thus increased.

svl> append [ [1,2], 3 ]
[1,2,3]
svl> append [ [1,2,3,4], [1,2] ]
[ 1, 2, 3, 4, [1,2] ]

svl> prepend [1,2]
[2,1]
svl> prepend [ [1,2,3,4], [1,2] ]
[ [1,2], 1, 2, 3, 4 ]
