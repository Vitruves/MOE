Logical Negation: not, notnot

Syntax

    flag = not v
    flag = notnot v

Description

Perform logical NOT operation on leaf elements of v. notnot is an accelerated form of not not.

    svl> not [2,0,-1]
    [0,1,0]
    svl> notnot [2,0,-1]
    [1,0,1]
